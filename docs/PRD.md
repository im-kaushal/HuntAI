# HuntAI Product Requirements

## Vision

HuntAI is an AI-powered Job Search Operating System designed to automate and optimize every stage of a software engineer's job search.

The goal is not to mass apply to thousands of jobs. The goal is to identify the highest-quality opportunities, maximize interview conversion, and reduce repetitive work while keeping the user in control of final submissions.

## Product goals

1. Universal job discovery from company portals, ATS platforms, job boards, professional networks where supported, and user-configured sources.
2. AI resume understanding that extracts a structured candidate profile from uploaded resumes.
3. Intelligent semantic job matching with explainable strengths, weaknesses, missing skills, confidence, and ATS compatibility estimates.
4. Unified job dashboard with normalized job metadata and AI recommendations.
5. Company intelligence covering size, funding stage, headquarters, tech stack, hiring trends, engineering news, interview difficulty, and response-rate estimates.
6. Referral discovery for user-authorized workflows with suggested reasons for outreach.
7. AI outreach assistance for referral requests, cold introductions, follow-ups, thanks, interview scheduling, and recruiter replies.
8. Role-specific resume optimization with version history and quality scoring.
9. Company-specific cover letter generation.
10. Application assistance with pre-fill, resume upload, common answer drafting, and a review screen before user-confirmed submission.
11. End-to-end job tracking across saved, interested, preparing, applied, OA scheduled, interview, offer, rejected, and withdrawn states.
12. Tailored interview preparation for each application.
13. Analytics for discovery, application conversion, resume performance, skill gaps, company response, and source effectiveness.

## Success metrics

- Reduce time spent searching by at least 80%.
- Surface a higher ratio of high-match opportunities.
- Reduce preparation time for each application.
- Improve interview conversion through stronger targeting and tailored application materials.
- Preserve an extensible architecture for providers, AI models, and workflows.
